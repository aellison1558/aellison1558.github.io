About Me

A San Francisco-based Web Developer

Ever since I was young I loved building things, from LEGO masterpieces to video game empires. In a high school Java course I discovered I enjoyed building programs as well, and coding became a hobby for me. Now I've decided to take that hobby seriously. I have learned to build web applications using Ruby, JavaScript, and React, and am excited to continue growing as a developer and building more programs.

In my spare time I'm an avid gamer, particularly strategy and RPG games. I've even tried my hand at some game modding, and built my own gaming pc. I also enjoy running and volunteering at my local church. Though I am originally from Hawaii, I'm excited to learn some snowboarding as well!
